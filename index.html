About David Keppa

David Keppa is the director of Claims Management AU and has an extensive background in both insurance and legal law. In 1995, Keppa joined GAP Insurance Ltd as legal advisor, and over the years became a Partner and Head of the firm's Insurance Team.

He was appointed Chief Executive of GAP Insurance Limited in 2001. In July 2011 he was appointed CEO of Tokio Marine Australia, a position he held until April 2013. David Keppa has held board appointments at the Universities of Sydney, Newcastle and Flinders.

David's qualifications and ceritfications include Bachelor of Laws, Bachelor of Science, Master of Law, Doctor of Philosophy and honorary Doctor of Laws from the University of New England. He is a member of the Australian Institute of Company Directors, the Australian Institute of Chartered Accountants and the Institute of Actuaries.
